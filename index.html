About Medic.AI

With a plethora of practicalities, Medic.AI solves the growing demand for means of effective remote healthcare and mental health resources. By enabling patients to book doctor's appointments, purchase medication and receive immediate medical diagnosis, Medic.AI is an all-in-one application that empowers everyone, everywhere.

Using Google Cloud and Google Analytics, Medic.AI was created with Dialogflow AI and the Google Cloud Console. To maximize the efficiency and optimization of the machine learning model, various datasets were used; encompassing extensive ranges of mental illnesses and medical diagnoses from Kaggle and AI intents.
